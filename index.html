Child Safety Standards - LocalBuzz

Last Updated: May 2024

LocalBuzz is committed to providing a safe environment for all users. We have a zero-tolerance policy regarding Child Sexual Abuse Material (CSAM) and exploitation.

1. Prohibited Content

Any content that depicts, promotes, or facilitates the sexual exploitation or abuse of children is strictly prohibited. Such content will be removed immediately upon discovery.

2. In-App Reporting & Moderation

LocalBuzz provides in-app reporting tools. Users can report any post or user by tapping the "Report" button. Our moderation team reviews all reports within 24 hours and takes appropriate action, including content removal and account termination.

3. User Blocking

Users have the ability to block any other user to prevent further interaction, contact, or visibility of content.

4. Law Enforcement Cooperation

We comply with all legal requirements to report CSAM to the relevant authorities, including the National Center for Missing & Exploited Children (NCMEC) and local law enforcement agencies.
